Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Welcome to Bryan’s Café's Privacy Policy. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting any personal data collected through our enquiry form and website. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

2. Information Collection

We collect information that you provide directly through our enquiry form, including your first name, last name, email address, and any additional details in your message.

3. Usage of Information

The information we collect is used solely to respond to your enquiries and provide you with relevant information about our services and updates about Bryan’s Café.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not share your personal data with third parties except where legally required or with trusted partners to assist in delivering our services to you, with confidentiality agreements in place.

5. Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Your data is stored securely and only accessed by authorized personnel.

6. User Rights

You have the right to request access to or deletion of your personal data at any time. Please contact us directly to exercise these rights.

7. Policy Updates

This privacy policy may be updated periodically. We encourage you to review this page regularly to stay informed about any changes.
